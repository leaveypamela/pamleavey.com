How is your artwork born?

I begin by opening the channel. I sit in ritual with your name and date of birth, draw tarot to meet your spiritual guides, and invite the five elements to speak through the work.

No photo. No history. Only presence, intention and trust. The colours arrive, the symbols emerge, and the painting takes form over weeks of quiet, intuitive work in London.

When it is complete, your artwork arrives with a full written soul reading, channelled affirmations and a certificate of authenticity.
